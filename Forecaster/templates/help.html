Help


  • Step 1: Select the departure airport
  • Step 2: Select the day of your upcoming flight (must be within 15 days)
  • Step 3: Click the submit button located under the date
  • Step 4: Check the delay probability shown in the box with the pink bar
  • Step 5: Check the recommended date shown in the box with the blue bar
  • Step 6: (optional) You can adjust any of the weather statistics shown by clicking the up or down arrows located in each box, or by manually entering decimals in a box and then selecting the submit button located under the visibility box
  • Step 7: (optional) Hover over the vertical bar chart to view the delay probability difference of the hovered airport compared to the rest
  • Step 8: (optional) Hover over the scatterplot circles to view the exact date and delay probability selected

This model should not be solely used to make decisions regarding flights, but should instead be used as an informative tool
